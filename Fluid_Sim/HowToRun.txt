How to run Fluid Simulation
$ Python3 fluid.py 'config file' 'color scheme'

Change the animation via document by deleting the word 'rotation' 
at the begining of the file.

Movie 1 line:
Python3 fluid.py config4.json magma

Movie 2 line:
Python3 fluid.py config3.json Greens

Movie 3 line:
Python3 fluid.py config2.json plasma

Movie 4 line:
Python3 fluid.py config1.json Reds

Movie 5 line:
Python3 fluid.py config.json jet
